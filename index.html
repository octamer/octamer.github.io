About

I currently work at ADM where I lead protein engineering efforts using both structure-based and machine learning approaches. My work has resulted in multiple patent applications and successful advancement of strain engineering projects towards commercialization.

Previously, I worked at Absci designing high-diversity libraries (10M+ variants) for therapeutic antibody discovery, and at Glycosyn engineering glycosyltransferases for human milk oligosaccharide production. My academic training at Northeastern University focused on cannabinoid receptor structure-function relationships.
