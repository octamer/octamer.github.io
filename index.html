About Me

Senior scientist with 8+ years of experience in biochemistry, protein engineering, and drug discovery. I have extensive expertise in high diversity library generation, high-throughput protein production, purification and characterization across diverse modalities including biologics, enzymes, and membrane proteins.

I have a strong track record of leading cross-functional teams in matrix environments, advancing therapeutics through discovery pipelines, and fostering development of junior scientists. My work spans from academic research on cannabinoid receptors to industrial applications in enzyme engineering and biologics development.

Areas of Expertise

Directed Evolution

Library design, screening strategies, and selection methods for engineering enzymes and proteins with improved activity, specificity, and stability. Experience with libraries exceeding 10 million variants.

Protein Engineering

Structure-based engineering using PyMol, AlphaFold, and Schrodinger. De novo protein design with Protein/LigandMPNN and RFdiffusion. Extensive experience in biologics, enzymes, and membrane proteins.

High-Throughput Screening

Development of HT protein production and characterization platforms. Experience with SPR (Carterra LSA), BLI, CE-SDS, DSF, and SEC for rapid evaluation of thousands of protein variants.

Biologics Development

End-to-end development of mAbs, multi-specifics, and antibody fragments. CDR grafting, expression optimization in bacterial and mammalian systems, and clinical-grade production processes.
